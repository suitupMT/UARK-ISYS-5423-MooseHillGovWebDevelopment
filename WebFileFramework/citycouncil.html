Town Council The town of Moose Hide Landing operates under a Mayor-Council form of government. In this system, the town Council is the legislative and policy-making body of town government. The town Council sets town policy by passing ordinances and resolutions. The Council also determines the budget and appoints and removes certain Board, Committee, and Commission members.
